Weekly dish

Fried eggplants

Plate for: 4 people

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 12 minutes

Ingredients:

4 eggplants
Salt
Pepper
4 spoons of flour and oil

Preparation

Wash the eggplants
Cut them into slices
Sprinkle them with salt
Let them dry for 30 minutes
Flour them, and fry them for 5 minutes in hot oil
