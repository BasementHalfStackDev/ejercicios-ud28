Weekly dessert

Mallorcan Sweet potatoes

Plate for: 4 people

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1kg of eggplants
  • 1/4 liter of honey
  • Plenty of oil

Preparation:

  • Peel the potatoes
  • Cut them in slices half a centimeter thick
  • Fry them with plenty of oil, slowly, so that they are all cooked evenly
  • Remove them once they have a gold-ish color
  • Serve them with a layer of honey on top and put them on a tray with a napkin
